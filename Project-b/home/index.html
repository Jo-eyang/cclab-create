Welcome to the visual journey of MUSIC!
You can immerse yourself in a multi-sensory experience, as sound transforms into different visuals across distinct scenes: rain, city, and night. With the help of p5.js, this website transforms the auditory into the visual, offering a fusion of art and technology.
Explore the dynamic relationship between music and visuals! Each scene is tailored to complement the mood of the accompanying tracks. Witness a symphony of colors and shapes that respond in real-time to the beats and melodies, enhancing your connection with the music!
Also, take an active role in activating your microphone to produce live input. Watch as the scene react to the sounds you generate. By doing so you could creat a personalized visual experience!
Whether you are a music enthusiast, artist, or simply curious, experience the convergence of technology and artistry as you explore the rain-soaked ambience, the rhythmic pulse of the city, and the mysterious allure of the night on this music visualization website!
